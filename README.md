# Alzheimer AI Companion

**Hackathon:** Void Hacks 7.0  
**Organized by:** Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya (SVVV), Indore  

---

## **Problem:**  
Early detection and monitoring of Alzheimer's disease is challenging, especially in rural or low-resource areas. Traditional methods are costly, slow, and inaccessible.  

---

## **Solution:**  
**AI Companion for Daily Monitoring & Early Detection**  
- Multi-modal: speech analysis + mini cognitive tests + optional smartphone behavior  
- Continuous monitoring with offline capability  
- Provides explainable AI insights to doctors and caregivers  

---

## **Features:**  
- Offline-capable for rural healthcare  
- Trend visualization for patient cognitive health  
- Gamified mini-tests and AI reminders for patients  
- Dashboard for doctors and caregivers  
- Explainable AI (SHAP / LIME) for risk insights  

---

## **Status:**  
MVP prototype planned. Code and models will be added after hackathon.  

---

## **Impact:**  
Affordable, scalable, socially impactful solution bridging diagnostic gaps in Alzheimer’s care.
